Boosting Confidentiality In Digital Sharing Images On Social Platform With Trust Based Approach

This project introduces a trust-based privacy-preserving mechanism for sharing co-owned photos on social networks. It anonymizes photos based on each user’s privacy loss and trust level, reducing privacy risks. The publisher adjusts a threshold to balance privacy and shared information. A greedy algorithm optimizes this threshold, offering effective privacy protection with minimal data loss. Simulation results confirm that this approach significantly mitigates privacy risks for users.
